Our Story

Plink Logistics was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize parcel delivery by making it faster, more reliable, and accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of logistics enthusiasts has grown into a nationwide network of dedicated professionals committed to connecting people and businesses across the country.

Today, we handle thousands of packages daily, from urgent business documents to precious family gifts, ensuring each delivery is treated with the care and attention it deserves.
