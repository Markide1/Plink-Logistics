Tracking Tips

  • Tracking numbers are usually 10-12 characters long.
  • Check your shipping confirmation email for the tracking number.
  • Updates may take 24-48 hours to appear after shipping.

Delivery Info

  • Standard delivery: 1-3 business days.
  • Express delivery: 1 business day.
  • Signature required for packages over Ksh. 5,000.
